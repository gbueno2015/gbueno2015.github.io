Summary

Guillermo studied at the Kellogg School of Management, where he proved himself a brilliant chemist with a specialty in X-ray crystallography. In my five years of professional career so far, I find myself to be most excited when I own a project from beginning to end and when I can use my creativity to solve challenging problems. I enjoy taking calculated risks and feel challenged when I can have an influence in all areas of a business, and not only in a specific unit or department.
